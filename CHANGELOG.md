## [1.0.0] — 2026-03-20

### Added
- Graded card inventory with support for PSA, CGC, BGS, and TAG
- Ungraded card tracking with grading submission workflow and grading-return conversion
- Sold history with realized profit, acquisition type, and sale notes
- Dashboard with portfolio stats, company breakdown, performers, top holdings, inventory aging, recent sales, and favorites
- Portfolio Value Over Time chart with manual snapshot saving
- Monthly Profit / Loss bar chart
- Deal Calculator — cost basis and profit projection at any market percentage
- Trade Evaluator — multi-card trade comparison with live inventory picker
- CSV import tool for one-time backfill from Google Sheets exports (PSA, CGC, BGS, TAG, SOLD, RAW sheets)
- Combined Trade + Cash sale support (partial trade / partial cash deals)
- Grading fee field tracked separately from purchase price
- Photo support for graded cards
- Favorites system with horizontal scroll on Dashboard
- Numeric grade sorting (10 → 9.5 → 9 … 1) in Inventory and Sold views
- Dark / light mode following macOS system appearance
- `run.sh` launcher — auto-creates venv and installs dependencies
